# Homework

The first three assignments build on the **ToolBench** dataset, progressively exploring multimodal representations of agentic tool-use data — from raw preprocessing through fusion and alignment to vision-language model fine-tuning. HW4 continues with reinforcement learning on top of the HW3 VLM setup, and HW5 shifts to building goal-directed AI agents.

| # | Topic | Key Techniques | Notebook |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Multimodal Data Preprocessing | Modality extraction, TF-IDF, t-SNE, perplexity analysis | [HW1](./hw1/adls_mmai_HW1.ipynb) |
| 2 | Multimodal Alignment & Fusion | Early/Late/Tensor/LMF fusion, contrastive learning | [HW2](./hw2/adls_mmai_HW2.ipynb) |
| 3 | Vision-Language Models | Qwen2.5-VL, LoRA fine-tuning, prompt engineering | [HW3](./hw3/adls_mmai_HW3.ipynb) |
| 4 | RL for VLMs (GRPO) | Group Relative Policy Optimization, TRL `GRPOTrainer`, rule-based rewards | [HW4](./hw4/Homework_4_GRPO_VLMs.ipynb) |
| 5 | AI Agents in the Wild | smolagents, custom tools, vision agents, observability/eval (Langfuse) | [HW5](./hw5/adls_mmai_HW5.ipynb) |
